Fried Rice

Indulge in the creamy perfection of our Chicken Alfredo. Tender, succulent chicken breast
is grilled to perfection and combined with a velvety, homemade Alfredo sauce.
Each forkful is a harmony of flavors as the rich and creamy sauce coats every strand
of perfectly cooked fettuccine. The dish is elegantly garnished with grated Parmesan cheese
and fresh parsley, adding an extra touch of decadence.With its savory chicken, luscious sauce,
and tantalizing aroma,our Chicken Alfredo is an exquisite Italian classic that will transport
your taste buds to culinary bliss.

Ingredients

  • olive oil (1 tbsp)
  • boneless chicken thighs (4 pieces)
  • fettuccine (300g)
  • butter (1 tbsp)
  • nutmeg (half a piece)
  • parmesan (100g)
  • parsley (chopped)

Follow these steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick frying pan over a medium high heat.
    Add the chicken thighs and fry for around 10 mins, turning half way,
    until they are golden brown and cooked through. Set aside to cool a little,
    then use two forks to shred.

  2. Bring a pan of salted water to the boil and add the pasta,
    cook for 1 minute less that package instructions. Whilst the pasta is cooking,
    add the butter to the frying pan over a medium heat, scraping the bottom a
    little to get any of the browned bits. Tip in the cream along with the nutmeg
    and bring to a simmer. Add the chicken back to the pan.

  3. Once the pasta is cooked, use tongs to transfer the pasta straight from the water
    into the frying pan with the cream mixture. Sprinkle most of the parmesan over
    and use the tongs to toss it all together, adding a splash of the pasta water
    if it looks a little stiff. Season well, then tip into bowls.
    Top with the remaining parmesan, a scattering of parsley, and black pepper.
